# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do’s                                                                             

Do keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your latest academic projects at VIT.   

Do use strong privacy settings to control who can see your personal life.          

Do think for a few seconds before posting or commenting on a heated topic.         

Do use a clean, professional profile picture for your academic accounts.         

Do credit original creators if you share their designs or coding snippets.         


## Don'ts

Don’t post unprofessional or controversial content that could hurt your future career.

Don’t share your real-time location or sensitive personal details publicly.

Don’t tag your batchmates in photos or videos without asking them first.

Don’t click on ""too good to be true"" giveaway links sent via DMs.

Don’t engage with internet trolls or bullies; it’s better to just block them.

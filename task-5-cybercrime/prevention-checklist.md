## Stay Safe Online: Student Prevention Checklist
1. The Golden UPI Rule: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. A PIN is only required to send money or check your own balance.
2. QR Code Caution: Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown individuals on WhatsApp or OLX, especially those claiming you will "receive a payment".
3. Enable 2FA: Activate Two-Factor Authentication on your VIT college email, LinkedIn, and all social media accounts to add an extra layer of security.
4. Check the URL: Before entering any login credentials, double-check the website address to ensure it is the official site and not a phishing clone.
5. Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking: Never log into your bank account or make UPI payments while connected to free public Wi-Fi in cafes or airports.
6. Verify Before You Click: If you receive an "urgent" email or SMS about your account being blocked, do not click the links. Contact the official customer care instead.
7. Use Strong, Unique Passwords: Avoid using easily guessable passwords like your name or registration number. Use a mix of symbols, numbers, and letters.
8. Privacy Audit: Regularly check your social media privacy settings to ensure your personal details and location are not visible to strangers.
